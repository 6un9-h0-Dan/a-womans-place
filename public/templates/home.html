A Woman's Place Inc. shelters and helps empower victims and survivors of domestic abuse to become safe, secure and self-reliant; and through education and collaboration, mobilizes our community to help prevent domestic violence.

Serving victims and survivors of domestic violence in Weld County by providing:

Safe Housing, Crisis Support, Counseling Services, Legal Advocacy, Child and Youth Programs Prevention and Intervention Counseling, Information and Referrals, 24 Hour Crisis Line, Outreach Services, and Education.

What is Domestic Violence (DV) ?

Any act that one person uses to dominate another person in an intimate relationship. For example:

  • Intimidation - looks or gestures, property damage, displaying weapons
  • Using Emotional Abuse - put downs, humiliation, degradation
  • Isolation - controlling when or if the partner can go out to see family or friends, extreme jealousy or possessiveness
  • Using Children - threatening to take the children away, relaying messages through the kids, harassing during visitations
  • Minimizing, Denying, and Blaming - saying the abuse never happened, blaming the victim, blaming others for their actions
  • Sexual Abuse - unwanted sexual contact, treating the victim like a sex object
  • Rigid Gender Roles - using male privilege, making all big decisions, treating the victim like a servant
  • Economic Abuse - preventing the victim from getting/keeping a job or taking money
  • Coercion and Threats - making or carrying out threats to harm the partner, threatening suicide or to leave, making the victim do illegal things or intimidate them to drop charges or lie in court
