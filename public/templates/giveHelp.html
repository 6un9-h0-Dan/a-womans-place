Ways You Can Help

  • Open a Dialogue: "Are you ever afraid of X's temper?" "I've noticed X, Y, Z lately, is there anything you want to talk about?"
  • Express your concern
  • Appreciate the danger and talk about how the victim can stay safe
  • Commit to unconditional support, even if you do not approve of the person's choices
  • Listen. Don't try to fix the situation
  • Tell the victim the abuse is not her/his fault
  • Don't judge or blame the victim
  • Don't give advice
  • Offer help and set clear boundaries
  • Ask questions about how she/he is feeling
  • Provide her/him with resources such as A Woman's Place
  • Don't allow her/him to believe that no one cares!

Don't Be Fooled

Abusers lie about the abuse- they will try to hide it, deny it, and make excuses. Take all signs or threats of escalation seriously, and don't believe the abuser's excuses!
