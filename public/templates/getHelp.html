How to Take Action

In an Emergency

  • Call the Police - 911
  • Go to a safe place - Safe shelter, friends, family, hotel
  • Get medical attention if necessary

Preparing to Leave

We do not require identification for you to access our services, but it is important to locate these documents when preparing to leave an abusive relationship, and have them available at a safe location.

Identification:

  • Driver's License
  • Birth Certificates
  • Social Security Cards

Financial:

  • Banking and Credit Information
  • Any Cash Available

Legal Papers:

  • Protective Order
  • House deed/lease or Rental Agreement
  • Permanent Resident Card/VISA
  • Passport
  • Divorce/Custory Papers

Medical:

  • Medications
  • Medical Records
  • Health Insurance Information

Other Considerations:

  • Transportation
  • Safe Times to Leave

Have an overnight bag with the above items readily available.
